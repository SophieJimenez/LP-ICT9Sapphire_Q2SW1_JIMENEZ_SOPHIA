MIRACULOUS LADYBUG: The Tales of Ladybug and Chat Noir


Ladybug, "The Superhero of Creation" 🐞

Behind the Mask Special Ability Kwami


Marinette Dupain-Cheng is a cheerful teenager in Paris who dreams of being a fashion designer and secretly fights crime as the superhero Ladybug.

Ladybug's "Lucky Charm" lets her summon special objects to solve problems in battles, making her the superhero of creation.

Tikki is Marinette’s kwami, a small magical creature that gives her the power to transform into Ladybug and helps her with advice and support during battles.

Chat Noir "The Superhero of Destruction" 🐾

Behind the Mask Special Ability Kwami


Adrien Agreste is a kind-hearted and charming boy in Paris who models for his father's fashion brand while secretly fighting alongside Marinette as the superhero Cat Noir.

Cataclysm is Cat Noir's powerful ability that lets him destroy anything he touches, but he must use it carefully due to its serious consequences.



Plagg is Adrien's kwami, a playful and mischievous creature that helps him transform into Cat Noir while providing support and guidance.
